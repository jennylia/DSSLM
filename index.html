About DSSLM

The DSSLM (The DS Sleep Learning Module) is meant to be a very visual and interactive web-based tool that incorporates real case study encounters and allows students “to play a role” in the care of a virtual patient.

How can we help medical students?

Our online learning module enables medical students to cover the various complex and chronic sleep and associated medical problems that are faced by people with Down Syndrome.
